At the heart of Borrow'd is trust.

Standard trust:

Standard trust is the default. Items that are marked as "Standard" are visible to the people in the groups you've joined. Use this for everyday items you're comfortable sharing more widely.

High trust:

High trust items can only be seen and borrowed by people in groups you've marked as High trust. Use this for items that are valuable, fragile, or personal — the things you only want to lend to the people you know well.

Note: Your trust settings are private — no one else can see which items or groups you've marked as High trust.
